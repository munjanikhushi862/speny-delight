Inspired by spain's raw, rich land

Connecting people to place through contemporary Spaniards cuisine, Speny crafts inspired dishes reflective of our country’s diverse landscape, history and culture.Our chefs work closely with farmers and suppliers to source the finest ingredients from across the nation.


With over 20 years of culinary history, Speny continues to challenge creative boundaries in the spirit of celebrating spain and its distinct provinces.
